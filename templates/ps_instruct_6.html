Let's have a practice!

On the top right corner of the screen, there will be a counter to show you how many points you've collected along the way.

Each time you catch an asteroid you will win points.

Each time you miss it, you will loose points.


After, you will have to complete a short quiz relating to the game.

You will need to answer all of the quiz questions correctly to move on in the game - otherwise we will bring you through the instructions and practice again.

So, please, pay attention!
